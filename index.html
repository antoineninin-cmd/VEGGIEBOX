About VEGGIEBOX

Discover the urban Garden !

Discover VeggieBox, the ultimate solution for urban gardening. Our city gardening kits are designed for people with limited space—perfect for apartments, balconies, or small kitchens. Enjoy grow vegetables at home: indoor fresh herbs without the hassle.

Our kits are compact, sustainable and easy to install. One box = everything you need.

  • Perfect for students & young professionals
  • Can be placed in the kitchen or balcony
  • Sustainable materials

What’s Inside Your VeggieBox?

In each kit, you get:

  • Organic seeds for vegetables and herbs
  • Eco-friendly, recyclable pots and materials
  • Step-by-step guides to help beginners get started
  • Access to the VeggieBox app to track growth and receive personalized tips
“ Whether you’re a beginner or an eco-friendly gardening kits, VeggieBox makes gardening easy, fun, and rewarding. ”
each urban gardening kit comes with everything you need to grow fresh vegetables and herbs at home
